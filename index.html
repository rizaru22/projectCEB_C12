What is CEB12?

CEB12 is a training institute engaged in Information Technology that focuses on Computer Training services, Website Development and IT Solutions. CEB12 was established on August 16, 2000 in Tangerang. With these three main areas, CEB12 has dozens of years of experience in providing IT training services to both the public and companies.
For the field of Computer Education, CEB12 Training Center already has many alumni working in local and multinational companies in the IT field.
